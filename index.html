TRACK B

Notion Hub

A duplicatable Notion workspace with live databases, tuned to your business.

  • Full command center (CRM, content calendar, media library, project management)
  • Claude Projects trained on your voice, team, tools, and strategy
  • Integrated with your existing SaaS stack (ManyChat, Later, etc.)

Best for: Solo operators, coaches, content creators, DTC brands, and small teams who want the full system without the custom-software overhead.

Not sure which is right for you? The discovery call figures it out. Most clients end up on Track B — Custom Build is for when a Notion hub won't cut it.
